# Code of Conduct

Be respectful, constructive, and focused on the technical work. Harassment, discrimination,
personal attacks, and publication of another person's private information are not acceptable.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and temporarily or permanently exclude participants
whose behavior harms the project or its community. Reports should be sent privately through the
security contact mechanism described in `SECURITY.md`.

This policy applies in all project spaces and when representing the project elsewhere.
